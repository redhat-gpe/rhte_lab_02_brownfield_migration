Brownfield Migration to OpenShift

This multi-product lab guides you through a migration of an existing application to an OpenShift Container Platform environment. The lab begins by having you determine a level of effort estimation for the migration. Afterwards, the legacy applications are migrated to OpenShift with a guaranteed quality of service to ensure reliability in production.

The lab is deployed on Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform and includes the following components:
  • Red Hat Migration Toolkit
  • Red Hat JBoss EAP
